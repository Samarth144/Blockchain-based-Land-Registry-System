Search Help
How to search for land records:
  • By Survey Number: Enter the full survey number (e.g., "L-2023-4567")
  • By Location: Enter district, street, or landmark (e.g., "District 12")
  • By Owner: Enter owner name or wallet address (e.g., "0x3F...2D4E")
What information is publicly available?

The public search portal shows basic land information including survey number, location, area, and current owner wallet address. Detailed ownership history and documents are only available to authorized users.
